About the Company
Petroleum World is a multinational firm that designs, manufactures and deploys advanced robotics systems for automation at warehouses, distribution and fulfilment centres. With deep domain expertise, world class hardware and software engineering and the passion to solve complex business problems, the company is disrupting the way logistics and supply chain processes across the world are being optimized. Founded in 1983, Petroleum World is headquartered in NEW DELHI with offices in India, Hong Kong, Japan, Germany and UAE, and a state-of-the-art Research and Development center in Gurugram , India.
